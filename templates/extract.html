Recommended: Network Tab

  1. Make sure you're logged in (see the desk map)
  2. Press Cmd+Option+I (Mac) or F12 (Windows)
  3. Go to Network tab
  4. Refresh the page (Cmd+R)
  5. Click on any request (e.g. "availability" or "booking")
  6. In Headers, find Authorization: Bearer eyJ...
  7. Copy everything after "Bearer " (the long eyJ... string)
Alternative: Console Command
  1. Open DevTools → Console tab
  2. Paste this command and press Enter:
(function(){for(let k of Object.keys(localStorage)){try{let o=JSON.parse(localStorage[k]);if(o.accessToken){copy(o.accessToken);return 'Copied!'}}catch(e){}}return 'Not found'})()
